Discover Historical Locations in Gujarat

Gujarat, a state in western India, has a rich and diverse history dating back to ancient times. Known for the ancient Indus Valley Civilization site of Dholavira, Gujarat has witnessed significant historical events that shaped India's cultural and economic landscape.

The region played a vital role in India's independence movement, with Mahatma Gandhi leading the Dandi March from Sabarmati Ashram. Gujarat is also home to majestic historical sites, including the Sun Temple of Modhera, Rani ki Vav, and Champaner-Pavagadh Archaeological Park, a UNESCO World Heritage site.

Explore Gujarat's historical maps to uncover hidden stories of its glorious past, trade routes, and architectural marvels.
